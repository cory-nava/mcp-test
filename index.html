Permit Types Available

Required for construction, renovation, or structural changes to buildings. Includes new construction, additions, alterations, and demolitions.

  • New construction
  • Additions and renovations
  • Structural modifications
  • Demolition permits

Required for electrical work and installations including wiring, panel upgrades, and new electrical systems.

  • Panel upgrades and replacements
  • New electrical installations
  • Wiring modifications
  • Commercial electrical work

Required for plumbing installations, repairs, and modifications to water and sewer systems.

  • New plumbing installations
  • Pipe replacements and repairs
  • Fixture installations
  • Water heater installations

Required for HVAC and mechanical systems including heating, cooling, and ventilation work.

  • HVAC system installations
  • Ductwork modifications
  • Ventilation systems
  • Commercial mechanical work
