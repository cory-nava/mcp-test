Frequently Asked Questions

Processing times vary by permit type:

  • Building Permits: 10-14 business days
  • Electrical Permits: 5-7 business days
  • Plumbing Permits: 5-7 business days
  • Mechanical Permits: 7-10 business days

Complex projects may require additional review time.

Required documents typically include:

  • Completed permit application
  • Building plans and specifications
  • Site survey or plot plan
  • Contractor license verification (if applicable)
  • Proof of insurance

Additional documents may be required based on project complexity.

Yes! You can track your permit application online using your application ID. Visit our Track Application page and enter your ID to see real-time status updates.

If your application needs revisions, you'll receive detailed feedback explaining what needs to be corrected. You can then resubmit your application with the necessary changes. No additional fee is required for resubmissions within 30 days.

For simple projects, you may be able to serve as an owner/builder. However, certain types of work require licensed professionals:

  • Electrical work must be performed by a licensed electrician
  • Plumbing work requires a licensed plumber
  • Structural work typically requires professional oversight

Check with our office if you're unsure about your specific project.

Permit Type Base Fee Additional Fees Notes
Building Permit $125.00 $0.50 per sq ft over 1,000 sq ft Includes plan review
Electrical Permit $75.00 $25.00 per additional panel Inspection included
Plumbing Permit $85.00 $15.00 per fixture Rough-in inspection required
Mechanical Permit $95.00 Based on BTU capacity Energy compliance review
